Best Survey Apps for 14 Year Olds ()

Finding a way to earn money at 14 can be challenging since most traditional jobs require you to be 16. However, several top-tier market research companies are specifically looking for the opinions of younger teens.

Why 14? Brands want to know what teens think about games, snacks, and technology. These apps allow you to earn without needing a bank account.

Top Verified Apps for 14-Year-Olds

1. Swagbucks (Age 13+)

The most flexible option. You earn "SB" points by taking polls, watching short clips, and playing mobile games. You can cash out for Amazon, Roblox, or Apple gift cards once you reach $3.

Join Swagbucks →

2. Toluna Influencers (Age 13+)

Toluna is great because it feels like social media. You can participate in fun battles and polls. It is very mobile-friendly for 14-year-olds.

Join Toluna →

3. LifePoints (Age 14+)

Now that you are 14, you qualify for LifePoints! They are known for very short surveys that only take 5 minutes, perfect for study breaks.

Join LifePoints →

How to Earn Without a Bank Account

Since most 14-year-olds don't have a personal bank account or PayPal, we recommend choosing Digital Gift Cards as your reward. You receive a code via email that you can use immediately on sites like Amazon or for Robux.
