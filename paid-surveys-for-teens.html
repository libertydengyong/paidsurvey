Best Paid Surveys for Teens (Ages 13-18)

Finding a part-time job as a teenager can be difficult. Luckily, many market research companies are eager to hear from younger generations. Online surveys are a perfect, flexible way to earn extra cash for games, clothes, or savings.

Pro Tip: If you are under 18, some of these sites will require parental consent during registration. Always be honest about your age!

Top Verified Survey Sites for Teens

1. Swagbucks (Age 13+)

Swagbucks is the most popular choice for teens. Beyond surveys, you can earn "SB" points by playing mobile games, watching short videos, and searching the web. Points can be traded for Amazon, Starbucks, or PayPal cash.

Min Payout: $3.00

2. Toluna Influencers (Age 13+)

Toluna is a fun, social-media-style platform. They often have quick polls and interesting surveys about brands you already use every day. It's very mobile-friendly for teens on the go.

Min Payout: $10.00

Join Toluna →

3. LifePoints (Age 14+)

LifePoints is known for its short, high-frequency surveys. It's one of the easiest ways to earn your first few dollars online. They pay out in PayPal cash and various retail gift cards.

Join LifePoints →

Safety Tips for Teen Earners

  • Never Pay to Join: Real survey sites are free. If a site asks for money, it is a scam.
  • Protect Your Identity: Do not share your bank password or Social Security Number.
  • Use a Separate Email: Keep your school and personal emails clean by creating a dedicated Gmail for surveys.
