🚨 Rule #1: Never Pay to Join

If a site asks for a "registration fee" or "account insurance," it is 100% a scam. Legitimate companies pay you for your time.

Red Flags to Watch For

  • Unrealistic Pay: No one pays $50 for a 5-minute survey.
  • Sensitive Data: Never share your bank passwords or Social Security Numbers.
  • Missing Payout Proofs: Always check our Verified Ledger before spending hours on a new platform.
