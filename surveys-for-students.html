🎓 Pro Student Earning Tips

  • Be Honest: Academic sites like Prolific will ban users who give random answers.
  • Check Daily: New surveys often pop up during business hours.
  • Cash Out Early: Once you hit the minimum (usually $5), cash out to keep your money safe.

Is it worth it?

While you won't get rich, taking surveys can easily cover your coffee, textbooks, or weekend snacks. It's the most flexible side hustle available for busy college students in 2024.
